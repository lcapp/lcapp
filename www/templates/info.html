Livecounts v1.1.0-beta

by @ColleensCuber

About

This app tells you how many subscribers YouTubers have in real time.

As you can probably tell, this app is a work in progress. Some features might break. Let me know if you find bugs or if you've got suggestions for improvements or feature requests. Have fun experimenting :)


Features

Live subscriber count: The most important feature of this app. The count updates every 2 seconds.

Share to Twitter: Share any YouTuber's live subscriber count to Twitter.

Search: Easily search for any YouTube channel. For very small channels, you may have to enter their URLs or channel IDs instead of their names.

Favorites: Save your favorite channels so that you don't have to search for them every time you open the app.

Notifications: Receive push notifications when your favorite YouTuber approaches or reaches a milestone.

Widget (NEW!): Check any YouTuber's subscriber count directly from your home screen. The widget updates every 30 minutes or when you tap the Refresh button.


How to get promoted on the app

There are three main ways to get promoted on the app:

Giveaway - Around 14 days of every month are up for grabs for anyone, as long as you have a YouTube channel. Simply enter by filling out the form on livecounts.net/giveaway.

Auction - Around 12 of the remaining days of that month are auctioned off. For more information, check livecounts.net/auction.

Mini-contests - Every month, mini-contests are hosted on Twitter. For information and updates, please follow @LivecountsSite.
